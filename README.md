üß† Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing the employee termination records of Brightness Ltd., a fictional retail organization.
The goal was to understand termination patterns, employee retention trends, and forecast the potential number of future terminations using Excel-based analytics.

Through this study, we aimed to answer key HR questions such as:

What proportion of exits were voluntary versus involuntary?

How can we estimate future terminations based on past trends?

üìà Key Findings

A total of 1,485 employees left the organization in 2020.

Layoffs formed the majority of separations (47.6%), followed by Retirement (26.4%) and Resignation (25.9%).

More than half of all exits (52.32%) were voluntary, indicating healthy career mobility but also possible retention issues.

Based on a 7% year-on-year growth projection, 2021 terminations were expected to reach approximately 1,589 employees.

The trend suggests the organization could face consistent turnover pressure unless proactive employee engagement and retention measures are implemented.
